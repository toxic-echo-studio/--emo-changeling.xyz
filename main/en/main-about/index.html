About us* (PL/EN)

The Reverse Emo Changeling

What's up! You've just stepped into The Reverse Emo Changeling base - a project resurrecting the MySpace vibe and the 2005-2015 era, back when the internet wasn't overrun by corporate bots and subcultures actually had a soul, not just a price tag. We're not blindly romanticizing the past, but we do miss having a real community, so we're doing things our own way - with better gear, but the exact same rebellion in our hearts.

We're not gonna write a whole novel about ourselves here—you can find all of that in our videos, vlogs, and podcasts. Putting it all here would just turn into a massive wall of boring text. Though, we're currently cooking up an "Unofficial" section for this site where we'll drop random facts and behind-the-scenes stuff about us over time. (Don't bother looking for it yet; we'll announce when it's live on Telegram!)

We want to breathe new life into a space that honestly just went dead. A ton of creators vanished from the web or slowly left the subculture behind. No drama, no shade—we just feel that void and plan to fill it with something that actually matters.

Our game plan? Solid, high-quality content deeply rooted in Emo and Scene vibes. But we're not just locking ourselves into music and threads. We want to dive into topics that actually hurt and matter—from mental health and empathy, to stuff close to the Queer community, and even some light activism.

Come build this safe space with us. Hit up our Telegram and check out our community tab on YouTube, because we're slowly getting our base up and running there. Real talk: YouTube is our main spot right now, and that's where we're pouring most of our energy. We're waiting for you.

What do we actually do?

We're not interested in empty reach or taking the easy way out for the algorithm. We do things that actually matter to us:

  • Video Production: We mix glitch/emo/scene vibes with modern quality. We don't do half-measures (even though we're still just learning).
  • Activism & Education: We are a voice of the progressive left, backed by facts and science. We educate about subcultures and push the importance of mental health hygiene.
  • Safe Space: We're building a place with zero room for hate and toxicity. If you're looking for a chill conversation, you're at the right spot.
  • Pigeon art: Yeah... pigeons. We turn artists and influencers into these flying rats and now we have a whole mini digital art collection—only for those we have a good vibe with, obviously. Sometimes people even call us 'Pigeon Boy' and honestly... kind of iconic.
