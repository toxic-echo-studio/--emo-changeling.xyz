What do we actually do?

We're not interested in empty reach or “taking the easy way out” for the sake of algorithms. We do things that matter to us:

  • Video Production: We blend glitch/emo/scene aesthetics with modern quality. We use solid software (DaVinci Resolve, LumaFusion, Blackmagic cam) and don't settle for half-measures.
  • Activism & Education: We are a voice of the progressive left, rooted in facts and science. We educate about subcultures and push the importance of mental health hygiene.
  • Safe Space: We're building a place where there's no room for hate. If you're looking for a respectful conversation, you're home.
  • Pigeon arts: Yeah… pigeons. We’ve been turning artists and influencers into these flying rats and now we’ve got a whole lil’ digital arts collection - only the ones we vibe with, obviously. Sometimes people even call us “Pigeon Boy” and honestly… kinda iconic.
